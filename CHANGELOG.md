# Changelog

## v1.0.0 (2026-03-10)

### 🎉 Initial Release

**Core Features**
- Drag-and-drop invitation card editor
- 8 pre-built templates: Wedding, Funeral, Birthday (4 free, 4 Pro)
- Text editing with font size and color controls
- Background color palette picker

**Typography & Design (Pro)**
- 8 premium Google Fonts: Dancing Script, Pacifico, Lobster, Montserrat, Oswald, Great Vibes, Raleway, Lato
- Full custom color picker for text and backgrounds
- Premium templates with exclusive designs

**Photo & Media (Pro)**
- Add photos from gallery or camera
- Image placement and resizing on canvas

**Export & Share**
- Export invitation as PNG image
- Share directly via any messaging or social app

**RSVP System**
- Create RSVP events with a unique QR code
- Guests scan QR to confirm attendance
- Real-time attendance list for the host

**Localization**
- Template content in 7 languages: English, Korean, Japanese, Chinese, French, Spanish, Arabic

**Monetization**
- Free tier with full editor and basic features
- Pro subscription: ₩4,900/month or ₩29,900/year (save 49%)
- Restore purchase support

**Ads**
- Banner ads on template selection (Free)
- Interstitial ad before export (Free)
